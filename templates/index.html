Emotivest

The purpose of this project is to determine whether the emotion of Twitter tweets is predictive of future stock prices

We used machine learning, aided by the Natural Language Toolkit, to interpret and map the sentiment of each stock's respective tweets

David Caulfield and Shaun Cox pursued this idea as their group project while at Byte Academy, a Python coding school, in New York, NY
